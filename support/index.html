Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rating system work?

PADLR. uses the Weng-Lin Bayesian algorithm to calculate your skill rating on a 0–7 scale. Your rating updates after each confirmed competitive match. The system is fair: beating higher-rated players boosts your rating more, while losing to lower-rated players costs more. Your confidence score improves as you play more matches.

How do I post a match result?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen. Select your partners and opponents, enter the set scores, and submit. Match posting takes under 30 seconds. All four players must have PADLR. accounts. The match will appear in the social feed once confirmed.

Do I need an internet connection to use PADLR.?

PADLR. works offline for core features like viewing match history and browsing your profile. Match posts and social interactions sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

What is PADLR. Pro and what does it include?

PADLR. Pro is a subscription that unlocks premium features: unlimited match history (free users see up to 10 recent matches), advanced statistics and rating history charts, data export (JSON format), and an ad-free experience. Subscriptions are available monthly or annually with a 7-day free trial. Manage your subscription in Settings.

How do I restore a Pro subscription I already purchased?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscription associated with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel, go to your iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > PADLR. Pro, and tap Cancel Subscription. You will continue to have Pro access until the end of the billing period.

How do I find partners to play with?

Go to the Discover tab to browse players near you. You can filter by rating range. Compatibility scores show how well you match up with each player. Tap "Invite to Play" on any player's profile to send a game invitation.

How do push notifications work?

PADLR. sends notifications for: match confirmations from opponents, rating updates after matches, game invitations, and social interactions (reactions, comments, new followers). You can customise which notifications you receive in Settings > Notifications.

How do I report or block another user?

On any match card or user profile, tap the three-dot menu (···) and select "Report" or "Block". Blocked users will not appear in your feed or discovery results. Reports are reviewed by our team.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Your account will enter a 30-day grace period during which you can log back in to cancel the deletion. After 30 days, all your data is permanently and irreversibly deleted, including your profile, match history, ratings, and social content. Your Apple ID authorisation is also revoked.

How do I export my data?

PADLR. Pro subscribers can export all match data in JSON format. Go to Settings > Export Data. This generates a file you can save or share.

What languages does PADLR. support?

PADLR. is available in English, Spanish (Español), Portuguese (Português), and Swedish (Svenska). The App Store listing is also available in French, Italian, and German.
