Privacy Policy

PADLR. by Rebel Lion Labs  |  Last updated: 2026-03-11

This Privacy Policy explains how Rebel Lion Labs ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the PADLR. mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account information: Email address and display name (required for account creation)
  • Profile information: Profile photo (optional), approximate location (city level, optional)
  • Match data: Match results, scores, participants, and match history you log in the App
  • Social data: Posts, comments, reactions, and follow relationships you create
  • Device information: Push notification tokens (FCM), device type and OS version for app functionality
  • Usage data: In-app interactions and feature usage for analytics and improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your padel skill rating using the Weng-Lin Bayesian algorithm
  • Enable partner discovery and compatibility matching
  • Deliver push notifications for match confirmations, rating updates, and social interactions
  • Display your profile and match history to other users
  • Improve the App through anonymised usage analytics
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Firebase / Google: Authentication, cloud database (Firestore), push notifications (FCM), and analytics. Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat: In-app subscription management and purchase validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob: In-app advertising displayed to free-tier users. Google Privacy Policy applies. You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With third-party service providers listed above, who process data on our behalf
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your consent for any other purpose

Your display name, profile photo, ratings, and match history are visible to other users of the App as part of the social features.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you request account deletion:

  • Your account enters a 30-day grace period during which you can cancel the deletion
  • After 30 days, all personal data (profile, matches, social content) is permanently deleted
  • Your Apple ID sign-in token is revoked immediately upon deletion request

You can request account deletion at any time from within the App under Settings > Account > Delete Account.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (see account deletion above)
  • Portability: Export your match data in JSON format (available in Settings > Export Data)
  • Object: Object to processing of your data for direct marketing purposes
  • Restrict: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at rebellionlabsofficial@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

PADLR. is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Data Storage Location

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in the United States (us-central region). By using the App, you consent to the transfer and storage of your data in the United States. We rely on Google's standard contractual clauses and data processing agreements to ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR Article 46. For more information about Google's data protection practices, see Google's Data Processing Addendum.

9. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely in Google Firebase.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
